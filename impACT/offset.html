Offset your carbon emissions!

Plant Trees!

A single mature tree can absorb 21.5kg of carbon dioxide per year! Planting trees can combat deforestation and improve air quality as well.

Walk more

You don't produce any carbon dioxide emissions by walking, and plus you get some healthy exercise.

Carpool!

Sharing a ride, or using public transport, greatly reduces CO2 emissions by, on average, over 2kg of carbon per 100km journey.

Fly less

If you cannot avoid flying, one way of making up for the emissions caused is to offset them by donating money to sustainable projects. Sometimes airlines will give you this option themselves, or you can use a third-party option.

Eat less meat

Some estimates suggest that a plant based diet has up to 100 times less of an environmental imapct compared to eating red meat once daily.

Recycle!

Americans generate about roughly 258 million tons of trash a year, 169 million tons of which ends up in landfills and incinerators! Recycle more to reduce emissions!
